INTRODUCTION:

Henan University of Urban Construction, located in Henan Province, was founded in 1983.There are 54 undergraduate majors, covering eight major disciplines including engineering, management, science, literature, law, and art. There are School of Civil and Transportation Engineering, School of Management, School of Municipal and Environmental Engineering, School of Architecture and Urban Planning, School of Energy and Building Environmental Engineering, School of Surveying and Mapping and Urban Spatial Information, School of Art and Design, School of Computer and Data Science, Electrical and Control School of Engineering, School of Materials and Chemical Engineering, School of Life Science and Engineering, School of Mathematics and Physics, School of Foreign Languages, School of Law, School of International Education, School of Marxism, School of Continuing Education and other 17 colleges, open to 31 provinces (municipalities, autonomous regions) and Enrollment of international students, there are currently 20,391 full-time students and 12,843 students of various adult education.
